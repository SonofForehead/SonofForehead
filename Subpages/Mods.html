BL Haki

BoneLab Haki is based off a show called One Piece, it's essentially a way to harden your skin making your punches more powerful and giving you better durability.

There's also Conquerors Haki, basically it knockes out any weak person within a large radius.

Finally Observation Haki allows you to see a little into the future and what your opponent will do, I could possibly add this, but I'd be very limited.
